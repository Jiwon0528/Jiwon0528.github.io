Paris Travel Guide

Introduction to Paris

Paris is the capital city of France. It is a major european city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. The city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré. Paris is also famous for its delicacy such as the baguettes and cheeses. Paris is a beautiful city and such a great place to travel. But before you travel this amazing place, there are some tips that will be useful during your trip. Follow along to learn about Paris!

Places to Visit

  • Louvre Museum
    •

    The Louvre Museum is a home to some of the world's most iconic pieces - paintings, sculptures, architectural elements and art objects by famous or anonymous artists of many different origins and eras. It is the largest museum on Earth. If you are interested in seeing the masterpieces of various artists,click here to learn more about the Louvre museum.

  • Eiffel Tower
    •

    The Eiffel Tower is probably the first thing when we think of Paris, or even France. Since it is the symbol of Paris, there are many Eiffel Tower Tour Programs. If you want to look around the beautiful lighting tower,click hereto learn more about the Eiffel Tower.

  • Versailles Palace
    •

    The Versailles Place was the principal residence of the French kings. Since kings used to stay there, it is fancy and gorgeous. it will surely be a special experience to look visit the palace that kings used to stay and see how kings and nobles lived back in the old days. If you want to visit the royal palace, click here to learn more about the Versailles palace.

  • Monet's Garden Bike Tour
    •

    Monet's Garden is a famous garden in Giverny, which is a village near Paris. It was created and maintained by the impressionist painter Claude Monet. This garden served as a major source of inpiration for many of his most celebrated works. You can even bike around this garden. If you want to get some background information before visiting this inspiring garden, click here .

  • Dinner Cruise on Seine River
    •

    Since France is the global center of art, rivers were also subjects of many artworks. The Seine River was a subject for many impressionist paintings. But did you know that you can have dinner on this river? If you ride the dinner cruise, you can have a marvelous dinner on this beautiful river. It would for sure be a precious memory to have dinner on the gorgeous river. If you want to know more about the Seine River, click here.
